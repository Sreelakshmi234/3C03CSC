KASARAGOD

Kasaragod is the northernmost district of kerala and is also known as saptha bhasha sangama bhumi

Kasaragod has the maximum number of rivers in kerala,all of them are west flowing river.Situated in the rich biodiversity of Western Ghats, it is known for the Chandragiri and Bekal Forts,[6] Chandragiri River, historic Kolathiri Rajas, natural environment of Ranipuram and Kottancheri Hills, historical and religious sites like the Madiyan Kulom temple, Madhur Temple, Ananthapuram Lake Temple and Malik Deenar Mosque.
