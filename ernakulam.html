ERNAKULAM

eranakulam district was part of the ancient kingdoms.Ernakulam District came into being in 1958 April 1 by incorporating Kanayannoor, Kochi, Kunnathunadu, Aluva and Paroor Taluks. Deliberation for hours held at the Kerala Fine Arts Hall in 1957 and attended by a host of political, social and media activists was instrumental in the formation of the District.

The samoothiri of kozhikode as well as the raja of travancore also atract the place eranakulam.
